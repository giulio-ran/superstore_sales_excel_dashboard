# # Supermarket sales Excel dashboard

## Introduction 
This dashboard is the one you can find in the Superstore_dashboard.xlsx Microsoft Excel file provided in this repository. It analyzes the sales dataset dividing the sales revenue by different criteria.

## Sections
1. The top-left section shows the sales by month, dividing its partial total by region (it is referred to US geagraphic macro-regions); november, december and march are the most profitable months, as it is well known in market trends.
2. The center section shows the top-5 months, farther zooming in the previous graph.
3. The top-right section shows the revenue by quarters, again we can observe that the last quarted of the year, Q4, is the most profitable., with a gradual ascending trend throughout each year.
4. The center-right section shows the sales by sub-category (top-8 sub-categories). It shows that, between the most profitable sub-categories, Chairs and Phones are at the first place (14% of total sales each), followed by Storage (10%). A substantial portion is covered by Others, meaning all the sub-categories that generated a lower revenue.
5. The two low-left sections show sales by category and sales by segment. For each variable, the most profitable were, respectively, Technology (37%) and Consumer (51%).
6. The center-low section shows the rank by cities. We can observe that New York, Los Angeles and Seattle were the best-performing ones.
7. The low-right section shows the revenue by region thoughout the years 2021-2024. During all the years, West and East outperformed Central and South, with a general ascending trend year by year.
